Miscellaneous

    • Tips

    • Hover over a task to get the delete and edit feature
    • To edit a task, type in the text you want to replace the current todo with in the "Add your Todo" bar, and press the edit icon
    • To give importance to a task, double click on the task
    • To change the color of the text for all tasks, press on the black strip and select the color you want
    • To sort your tasks in alphabetical order, press "Sort List"; it only works when there are two or more tasks
